Biography



My name is Sherry. I am a junior at the Pennsylvania State University studying Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics. I chose to pursue Computer Science because I am interested in incorporating technology into everyday life. More specifically, my interests lie in application development and fintech. A short-term goal of mine is to become a software engineer after I graduate. In fact, this summer, I am interning at Prudential Financial as a software engineer for the company's Global Technology division. A long-term goal would be transitioning into a business or leadership role within tech. If I do switch to business, I would also like to get an MBA.

An accomplishment I am proud of is my current teaching assistant position for data structures. Despite all the rigor in computer science, data structures was my favorite class as a freshman and what drove me to commit to my major. Hence, it is an honor to be a TA for the course and to be able to inspire and help other undergraduate students pursue degrees in engineering.

Over the past year, I have also worked on a variety of web development projects. Some applications I have built include a book review platform, a chat room, and my own personal website. These projects reflect my skills in full-stack development. With my knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Flask, and React, I believe I am a strong candidate for web and application development positions.
